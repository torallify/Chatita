## ğŸ“¸ Quick Recording Tips

**Slow Down!**
- Hold each screen for 3-5 seconds
- Let viewers absorb what they're seeing
- Don't rush button taps

**Make It Smooth:**
- Practice the flow 2-3 times
- Do multiple takes
- Choose the best one

**Show, Don't Tell:**
- Let the AI analysis finish completely
- Show real results, not mockups
- Demonstrate actual functionality

## ğŸ¤ Voiceover: Record Separately or Live?

**Option 1: Record Separately (Recommended)**
- More control over pacing
- Can re-record audio without re-filming
- Easier to sync in editing

**Option 2: Record Live**
- More natural feel
- Faster production
- Risk of mistakes

**My Recommendation:** Record screen first, add voiceover in editing.
